Chairman's message

Welcome to Learning Tree Academy. I appreciate your interest in our value-based high-achieving school. I am sure you and your child/children will enjoy the strong sense of community at the Learning Tree Academy. The focus of true education is to bring enlightenment. It equips us to understand ourselves, our roles and responsibilities to fellow human beings, society, country, and humanity. An educated individual is an asset to our society. Each one of us are born with great potential and special qualities by the divine. Learning Tree Academy aims to recognize this and prepare our students to realise and expand their latent talents so that they shine in their areas of strength. Eventually, the aim is for the students to become the role models for others to follow and be the best citizens of our country and take the responsibility of nation-building. Students in urban areas have the advantage of good schools that are equipped with the latest learning aids and competent teachers as their families can often afford to pay the fees of such premium institutions. In a rural area like ours, we lack such amenities, and our students are deprived of the facilities to compete with their urban counterparts. Our school aims to bridge this gap. We will take care of each child and provide them the required enriching environment for individual development.

We aim to develop our students academically, morally, and ethically so that there is all-round development that will set them apart and make them unique. We assure you that your children are in safe hands. We believe that they are destined to be confident individuals who can deal with the challenges in an optimistic, hopeful, and self-assured manner. They deserve a better world than ours and Learning Tree Academy promises to provide them with a stimulating learning ecosystem. Finally, to quote a famous Chinese saying: “If you are planning for a year, sow rice, if you are planning for a decade, plant trees, if you are planning for a lifetime, educate your people.” The Learning Tree Academy aims at development spanning a lifetime and longer.

I warmly invite you to be a part of our high achieving Learning Tree Academy.

Sincerely

Learning Tree Academy

At Learning Tree Academy, we aim to follow an educational system which facilitates all-round growth of a child’s personality. We impart education as envisioned by the Board of Directors of the School, who are personally linked to the institution on more levels than one. We aim to build a holistic personality – a personality that creates a harmonious balance of the different elements that constitute our being – our body, our thoughts, our feelings and impulses, our inspiration and our seeking
